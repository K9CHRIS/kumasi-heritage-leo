Who We Are

Empowering Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Sponsored by Lions Clubs International, the Leo Club Program provides youth with an opportunity to serve their communities and build essential leadership skills.

Our Kumasi Heritage Virtual Leo-Lions club bridges the energy and creativity of Leo youth with the experience and network of Lions. Together, we identify community needs and execute high-impact service projects.

🏅

Leadership

Lead projects, coordinate activities, and manage teams to build essential life skills.

⭐

Experience

Learn the value of cooperation and experience the joy of community service firsthand.

🤝

Opportunity

Connect with a global network of volunteers and discover personal growth paths.

Focus Areas & Service Projects

We channel our resources and efforts into three critical areas of community needs.
